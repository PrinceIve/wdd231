FoodKage - Site Plan

Site Name

FoodKage: A simple, anime-inspired online restaurant experience.

Reason for Name: The name is a play on “Hokage” from Naruto, suggesting food royalty with a fun, anime twist. It’s memorable, unique, and culturally intriguing for anime fans and food lovers alike.

Optional Domain: foodkage.com

Site Purpose

FoodKage is a casual online restaurant offering affordable, delicious anime-style meals starting with Caribbean rice and pancakes with chicken filling. The website aims to:

  • Allow customers to explore and order meals online.
  • Build a fun food culture around simple, hearty recipes.
  • Offer a smooth mobile-first ordering experience.

Scenarios

  • “Can I order Caribbean rice with extra chicken?”
  • “Do you deliver to Festac, Lagos?”
  • “What's in the FoodKage Pancake combo?”

Color Schema

  • Primary Color: Kage Orange (#F57C00): Naruto-inspired orange, bold and vibrant — used for buttons, links, and accents.
  • Secondary Color: Jet Black (#000000): Rich black for backgrounds and hero sections, gives a strong base feel.
  • Accent Color: Charcoal Black (#212121): Slightly softer black used in cards, overlays, and content blocks.
  • Neutral Color: Smoke Grey (#BDBDBD): Balanced grey for text, icons, and subtle lines.
  • Light Color: White (#FFFFFF): High contrast for text and highlights on dark backgrounds.

Typography

  • Heading Font: "Poppins", sans-serif: Energetic and modern.
  • Body Font: "Open Sans", sans-serif: Clean and highly readable.
